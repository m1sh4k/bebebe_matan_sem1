$\exists N:\forall n>N$
$a_{n}\leq b_{n}\implies a\leq b$

## Докво

Пусть
$\lim_{ n \to \infty }\{ a_{n} \}=a$
$\lim_{ n \to \infty }\{ b_{n} \}=b$
$a\leq b$
$\varepsilon:= \frac{{a-b}}{3}$

Тогда
$\exists n_{\varepsilon}':\forall n>n_{\varepsilon}'$
$|a_{n}-a|<\varepsilon$


$\exists n_{\varepsilon}'':\forall n>n_{\varepsilon}''$
$|b_{n}-b|<\varepsilon$

$\forall n>\max\{ N,n_{\varepsilon}',n_{\varepsilon}'' \}$
$a_{n}\in U_{a,\varepsilon}$
$b_{n}\in U_{b,\varepsilon}$

$$
a_{n}\leq b_{n}\implies 
\left\{
\begin{aligned}
&U_{a,\varepsilon}\cap U_{b,\varepsilon}=\emptyset\implies a<b\\
&U_{a,\varepsilon}\cap U_{b,\varepsilon}\neq \emptyset\implies U_{a,\varepsilon}=U_{b,\varepsilon}\implies a=b
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
